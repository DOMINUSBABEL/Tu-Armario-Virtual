# 🌟 DY(DressYourself) Vision & Gamification

Our goal is to build an application that stands alongside top fashion-gamification apps like *Dress to Impress*.

### Core Pillars
1. **Fluid UI/UX:** Leveraging Compose Multiplatform, animations must be 60fps, utilizing standard transitions (fadeIn, fadeOut).
2. **Neon & Cyber Aesthetics:** Dark backgrounds (`#1A1A2E`), hot pink accents (`#xFFFF69B4`), and neon vibes.
3. **Engaging Loops:** Give players immediate feedback via Badges, Style Points, and Global Leaderboards.
4. **Accessibility First:** High contrast themes must still be readable and follow material design guidelines for touch targets.
